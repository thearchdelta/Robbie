Robbie Tucker came to NYC from California where she worked at Francis Coppola's Zoetrope in San Francisco and Los Angeles. Knowing her lifelong love of spinning yarns, friends suggested maybe she should write them?

What she discovered was how much she loves helping other writers tell their stories. A background in film production and time in editing rooms inform her keen awareness of details and continuity.

She has a sharp focus, she listens carefully, and she heartily embodies your characters and plot lines. With cinematic vision she helps writers create a movie in the minds of their readers.
